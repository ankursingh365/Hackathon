ABOUT THE NGO

Manorama Dabral Jan Kalyan Samiti is a community and rural based NGO, founded and incorporated on the 19th September 2015 with registration number UK061808112020005305 and working for the promotion of Healthcare, Educations, Child Labour, Women Empowerment, Environment, Agriculture, Water conservation, Prevention of Domestic Violence, Supporting orphanages & providing help in other social causes.
